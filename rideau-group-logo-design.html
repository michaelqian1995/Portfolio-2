ABOUT LOGO

The left side of the logo features a royal lion as the main element. The image of the lion represents authority, professionalism, and strength, highlighting the professionalism of the team. On the right side of the logo is the letter R, taken from the initials of the team name and occupation.

The logo uses gold and blue as the main colors, which adds a sense of business to the team. According to customer requirements, I designed three different types of logos to adapt to different use environments.
